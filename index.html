About Us

Government Polytechnic Barauni, Begusarai is the primer institute of the state imparting diploma level education in different fields of engineering situated in the industrial most developed town. Government Polytechnic Barauni, Begusarai is situated amidest big near by Industries like Refinery of I.O.C. Fertilizer plant of H.F.C., B.T.P.S. and various small scale, medium scale industries. It is located on N.H.-31 at a distance of about 3 KMs west of the nearest Rly. Station, Begusarai (N.E. Railway). Main nearest town is Begusarai having its Geographical location as latitude 25.5 N. and longitude 86.2 East....Our Honorable founder has dedicated this institute to empower technical education of the country to achieve quality education imbibed with technical, professional and ethical values. At Government Polytechnic Barauni, we provide high-end diploma program in several branches i.e. Civil Engg, Chemical Engg., Electronics Engg. And Computer Science & Engg. Institute has been provided with qualified and experienced teaching faculty, well equipped laboratories, spacious library, playgrounds and all other needful infrastructural facilities. Our continuous and constant striving to provide finest teaching-learning environment is yielding excellent results in the examinations and in turn those students are performing well in examinations of real life also. We are in the pursuit of global standards of excellence in all our endeavors and we aim at creating all rounded personality in our students professionally and socially. In order to co-operative with increasing manpower of skilled personnel, visualizing the growing demand of Data Entry Operators in almost all business organization this institution has also run another short term program like I.R.G. Scheme, BSDM, CDTP and PMKVY. I welcome the student community to this Institute, join them and avail an opportunity to have a meaningful career and become responsible and respectable citizens of modern India.
